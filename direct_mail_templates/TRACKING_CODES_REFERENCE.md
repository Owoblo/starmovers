## CALCULATING ROI BY CAMPAIGN

### Formula:
```
Campaign ROI = (Revenue from Code - Cost of Campaign) ÷ Cost of Campaign × 100
```

### Example:
- DL25 Campaign: 45 letters × $2/letter = $90 cost
- Revenue from DL25 bookings: $1,200
- ROI = ($1,200 - $90) ÷ $90 × 100 = **1,233% ROI**
